## Question:
Input a integer n,
Output all the digits of the integer n in inverse other
> **Hints:** Use ***IF*** and ***WHILE*** to do it

## Example 1:
### Input:
> 123
### Output: 
> 3
> 
> 2
> 
> 1

## Example 2:
### Input:
> 10
### Output: 
> 0
> 
> 1

## Example 3:
### Input:
> 201
### Output: 
> 1
> 
> 0
> 
> 2

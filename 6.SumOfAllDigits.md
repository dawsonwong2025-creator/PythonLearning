## Question:
Input a integer n,
Output the sum of all the digits of the integer n
> **Hints:** Use ***IF*** and ***WHILE*** to do it

## Example 1:
### Input:
> 123
### Output: (1+2+3=6)
> answer: 6

## Example 2:
### Input:
> 10
### Output: (1+0=1)
> answer: 1

## Example 3:
### Input:
> 201
### Output: (2+0+1=3)
> answer: 3
